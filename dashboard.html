üìö Instructions for LLM Integration

The Virtual Company system is designed to work with AI language models. Here's how to connect:

1. Role-Based AI Prompts

Each role you create has custom AI instructions. When communicating with an AI model:

  • Select the role from the chat interface
  • The AI will respond according to that role's instructions
  • Use specific, clear prompts for better responses
